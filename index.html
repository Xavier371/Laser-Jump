How to Play

  • Move: Arrow Keys (desktop)  ·  Joystick on right side (mobile)
  • Jump: Space (desktop)  ·  Tap anywhere on left side or grey button (mobile)
  • Phase through any wall to teleport to the opposite side
  • Collect coins to score — each coin in Hard mode spawns a new laser
  • Use the Speed slider to adjust how fast lasers move
