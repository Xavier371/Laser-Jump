How to Play

Desktop:

  • Arrow Keys: Move the blue dot.
  • Spacebar: Jump over lasers.
  • Enter/Space: Restart when the game is over.
  • P: Pause/Resume the game.

Mobile:

  • Tap and Hold: Tap and hold on the sides of the screen to move.
  • Jump: Tap with two fingers, or tap once while moving.
